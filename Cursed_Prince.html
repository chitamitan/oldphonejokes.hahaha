Cursed Prince

by Unknown

Posted on October 31, 2016 at 10:00 PM [First received on [mm dd, yyyy]]

A prince was cursed by a witch.

He could only speak a word per year.

But if he doesn't, he could save the words for the next year.

One day, he saw a pretty princess and fell in love with her.

He waited for 9 years to say,

"Milady, I love you. Will you marry me?"

At ang sabi ng prinsesa...

"Pardon?"
